Use time well

A few tips on this site. Take or leave as you wish

Trust the timer. Ideally avoid looking back on it. Pay attention as you click. You'll see a RUNNING message on the tab, that way you know you did start - but you dont know time remaining. The reason for this, is knowing how long left changes my focus. But also looking at the time is breaking from the task.

It also tracks your distractions. Every time you focus on the tab during the timer, it counts as a distraction. So if you want, you can use this as a proxy measure. So for example, every time I look at facebook, I tab into this page to 'log' a distraction.

Originally I avoided music, but now I do. I find I enjoy my work more, if I need to focus I use one of the three links at the bottom. Especially heavy mental work. My rule is never change my spotify playlist in a work cycle. And if I do, I count that as a distraction. At times working in silence is surprisingly pleasant if you can.

When a cycle is complete, you'll here a small ding. I've kept it unintrusive. But it's amazing how good you get at picking it out. I wanted something pleasant, nice but also which will let you keep going if you want. Just click the extend button if you continue working and it'll log that cycle as extra long.

With tracking. Be honest, and be slightly pedantic. Aka, the distraction counter might miss something or you might waste the last minute before the timer. If there's something you don't quite know if you should count it as a distraction.Count it. If you think you missed out a counter, add it. It's better to be slightly pedantic on the rules rather than allowing grey areas, because it stops you cheating them in the long run. For me personally, I don't do any rewards or punishments based on this. It's just a factual record of my work. No judgement, just guidence, and a record to help me see how I am working and how I could improve. This way, there's no incentive to cheat. But also, it's easier to keep up, as it's not a source of shame.

Reviews of the three noises I've chosen

Coffivity Coffinity - sound of cafe, more loud. Very effective if you're working in a noisey place, as it makes everything blur. Also if you're working alone, makes you feel a bit less isolated. Very occasionally distracting. My more extroverted work music.

Nosili Noisli - Mix and match sounds from the site. Also has a timer too. Need an account to access, and can be a bit distracting to find the perfect mix of sounds. More if you want to get some space and feel a bit more alone. My more introverted work music. Top combo - rain, thunder and brown noise.

Lofi hip hop mix Lofi hip hop mix - nice, main risk is this is a youtube video and the video is a bit distracting. But is nice and relaxing to work to. 2 hours of music. Makes you feel like you're in one of the panoramic shots by Johnny Harris for Vox.
